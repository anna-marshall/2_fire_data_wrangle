Q1. Correlation between NDVI and NDMI

The NDVI is correlated with the NDMI. A higher NDMI should result in a higher NDVI. In other words, more moisture will result in a higher amount of greeness. More precipitation and wetter soils means vegetation can grow more effectively. However, we see a different relationship post-burn.

July appears to be the greenest month on average in both the burned and unburned zones.
